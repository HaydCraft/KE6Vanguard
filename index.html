VANGUARD

King edward vi college Esports Team

ABOUT US >

KE6 Vanguard is the personal Esports team of King Edward VI College We compete in British Esports under Student Championship Competitors in League, Valorant, Rocket League & Overwatch 2 Face The Sword!

HISTORY >

Ke6 Vanguard was established as the team on the Esports course back in 2022 by first year students of Esports at KE6. Has teams in League of Legends, VALORANT, Rocket League and Overwatch 2. Since then the team has claimed countless victories for the college showcasing themselves as a fierce competitor within the industry and is now a part of the KE6 Sports Performance Academy, offering students expert guidance into competitive grassroots esports.
